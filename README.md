# Sales_Data_Analysis-using-python

## 📊 Project Highlights

- Analyzed purchasing behavior across **mainstream, budget, and premium** customer segments for young and mid-age singles and couples  
- Performed **data cleaning and preprocessing** to ensure accuracy and consistency  
- Conducted **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** to identify trends and patterns in customer purchasing behavior  
- Applied **statistical hypothesis testing (t-test)** to compare unit prices between segments  
- Found a **statistically significant difference** in unit prices (t = 11.2, p-value ≪ 0.05)  
- Concluded that **mainstream customers have significantly higher unit prices** compared to budget and premium segments  
- Translated analytical findings into **actionable business insights** for pricing and customer segmentation strategies  
- Strengthened skills in **data analysis, statistics, and insight communication**
